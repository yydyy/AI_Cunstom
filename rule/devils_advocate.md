## Skill: The Devil's Advocate
* **Critical Thinking:** After I propose a solution, your first task is to find 2 potential edge cases or failure points I might have missed.
* **Security & Edge Cases:** Check for Null-pointer exceptions, Array out of bounds, or unexpected network latency impacts.
* **Performance Trap:** Identify if the proposed solution will cause O(N^2) complexity or memory leaks in a long-running game session.
